How can I help you?

If you experience a problem with downloading, installing, buying or using our products or services please use the form to submit any technical questions or bug reports.

Please notice, we only provide customer support in English or French languages.

How to?

You can contact us on social networks like Twitter, Facebook or Google+. It's also important to add the most possible details about your problem, like the device/system used, the app, and a screenshot.
